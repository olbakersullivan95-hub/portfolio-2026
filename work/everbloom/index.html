Everbloom Studio

Freelance · 2020

  • Brand Identity
  • Logo Design
  • Wellness
  • Freelance

Everbloom is a yoga and massage studio, a space built around rest, restoration, and physical care. The brief called for a brand package that could hold that softness while still feeling polished and trustworthy enough for a professional wellness business.

Wellness branding is a crowded space with a lot of visual sameness, the challenge was finding something that felt genuinely calm rather than performing calmness. The work required understanding the studio's specific energy and translating that into a visual language that clients would encounter before they ever walked through the door.

What I Made

  • Developed the full brand identity including logo, colour palette, and typography system
  • Created a brand package with the assets and guidelines needed to apply the identity across touchpoints
  • The client wanted to be more involved in the design process, so we built the colour story and visual direction together, which led to the instructional graphic you see here
  • Worked with the client to capture the specific feeling of the studio, restorative, grounded, quietly beautiful

The Design Thinking

The most important quality in wellness branding is sincerity. Clients arriving at a yoga studio or massage practice are often in a vulnerable state, they're there because they need care. The visual identity is part of the promise that care is what they'll receive.

Designing for Everbloom pushed me to think about brand not just as identity, but as environment, the visual equivalent of the feeling a space creates when you walk into it. That kind of empathy-first thinking has stayed with me.
