VPOS Pages

Block / Square · 2024

  • +33% Bottom-of-Funnel Conversion
  • 500M+ Annualized Incremental NVA
  • Global Multi-locale Launch
  • Conversion Optimization
  • Animation
  • Localization

VPOS (Virtual Point of Sale) pages were among Square's highest-stakes public web properties, product pages directly tied to merchant acquisition and bottom-of-funnel conversion. These pages needed to work across multiple locales, devices, and use cases, with tight coordination between design, engineering, and product marketing.

My contribution spanned the full production lifecycle: from slicing and building assets to leading QA rounds, supporting A/B test setup, and ensuring localized variants were accurate and launch-ready.

What I Did

  • Built and sliced production-ready assets across desktop and mobile breakpoints
  • Designed and implemented original hero animations that shipped to the live page
  • Supported A/B testing setup, including experiment link creation and variant QA
  • Led localization across multiple markets, ensuring design integrity was maintained in every locale
  • Stepped in to lead QA rounds when the primary production designer was out of office
  • Coordinated directly with developers to ensure CMS implementation matched design intent

The Systems Thinking

Conversion improvements come from a lot of small things going right: the copy, the imagery, the animation, the localized variants, all executed carefully and consistently. When the lead PD was out, I picked up the QA and kept things on track.

There's something I genuinely like about that kind of ownership, where the work shows up in the results even when your name isn't attached to them.
