intro: |
  Everbloom is a yoga and massage studio, a space built around rest, restoration, and physical care. The brief called for a brand package that could hold that softness while still feeling polished and trustworthy enough for a professional wellness business.

  Wellness branding is a crowded space with a lot of visual sameness, the challenge was finding something that felt genuinely calm rather than performing calmness. The work required understanding the studio's specific energy and translating that into a visual language that clients would encounter before they ever walked through the door.

conclusionHeading: The Design Thinking
conclusion: |
  The full brand identity was developed, logo, colour palette, typography system, and brand package, in close collaboration with the client to understand the specific feeling of the studio. The most important quality in wellness branding is sincerity. Clients arriving at a yoga practice or massage studio are often in a vulnerable state. The visual identity is part of the promise that care is what they'll receive, and it has to mean it.

  That project reframed brand not just as identity but as environment, the visual equivalent of the feeling a space creates when you walk in. That empathy-first thinking has stayed across every project since.
