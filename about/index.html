Hi there! My name is Olivia Baker-Sullivan.

A little more about me: My background is interdisciplinary. I started in visual arts and music, got my Master's in Interdisciplinary Design, and that's really where I shifted from pure aesthetics into systems thinking, research, and strategy. That foundation is still very much how I approach product work today.

In my early career, I worked across branding and agency environments: Healthcare nonprofits, startups, and then as a designer on the eBay account at an agency, where I built my instinct for designing within established systems and moving fast in cross-functional teams.

The last few years I was at Block (Square's parent company) I was embedded on a web and product team where marketing and product experience were deeply connected. In practice, that meant all designers bridging web surfaces with in-product journeys, working directly with PMs and engineers on acquisition, onboarding, and global launches.

Outside of work, I play violin and volunteer with the Strings Attached Orchestra, I volunteer with the RGD on the D&I committee to give back to the design community that gives to me, and I am presently enjoying a combination of hot yoga and Muay Thai for some activity.

Want to work with me, or just a query? Let me know!
